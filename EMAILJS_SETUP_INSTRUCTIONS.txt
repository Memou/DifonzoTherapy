=====================================
EmailJS Setup Instructions for Contact Form
=====================================

Follow these steps to make your contact form send emails:

STEP 1: Create an EmailJS Account
----------------------------------
1. Go to https://www.emailjs.com/
2. Click "Sign Up" and create a free account
3. Verify your email address


STEP 2: Add an Email Service
-----------------------------
1. In your EmailJS dashboard, go to "Email Services"
2. Click "Add New Service"
3. Choose your email provider (Gmail recommended)
4. Follow the prompts to connect your email account (melissa@difonzotherapy.com)
5. Note down the SERVICE ID (e.g., "service_abc123")


STEP 3: Create an Email Template
---------------------------------
1. Go to "Email Templates" in the dashboard
2. Click "Create New Template"
3. Set the template as follows:

   Template Name: Contact Form Submission
   
   Subject: New Contact Form Submission from {{from_name}}
   
   Content (Body):
   ---------------
   You have received a new message from your website contact form.
   
   Name: {{from_name}}
   Email: {{from_email}}
   Phone: {{phone}}
   
   Message:
   {{message}}
   
   ---------------

4. In "To Email" field, enter: melissa@difonzotherapy.com
5. Save the template
6. Note down the TEMPLATE ID (e.g., "template_xyz789")


STEP 4: Get Your Public Key
----------------------------
1. Go to "Account" > "General"
2. Find your "Public Key" (e.g., "AbCdEfGhIjKlMnOp")
3. Copy this key


STEP 5: Update Your Contact Page
---------------------------------
1. Open the file: contact.html
2. Find these three lines near the bottom (around line 120):

   const EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY = 'YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE';
   const EMAILJS_SERVICE_ID = 'YOUR_SERVICE_ID_HERE';
   const EMAILJS_TEMPLATE_ID = 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID_HERE';

3. Replace them with your actual values:

   const EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY = 'AbCdEfGhIjKlMnOp';  // Your public key from Step 4
   const EMAILJS_SERVICE_ID = 'service_abc123';    // Your service ID from Step 2
   const EMAILJS_TEMPLATE_ID = 'template_xyz789';  // Your template ID from Step 3

4. Save the file


STEP 6: Test Your Form
-----------------------
1. Open your website's contact page
2. Fill out the form with test information
3. Click Submit
4. You should receive an email at melissa@difonzotherapy.com
5. Check your spam folder if you don't see it immediately


TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------
- If you get an error, check the browser console (F12) for details
- Make sure you copied the IDs correctly (no extra spaces)
- Verify your email service is properly connected in EmailJS
- EmailJS free plan allows 200 emails per month
- If you need more, upgrade your EmailJS plan
