Our Founder

Melissa Di Fonzo, MSW, RSW

Founder & Clinical Director

Di Fonzo Therapy & Associates was founded by Melissa Di Fonzo, a Registered Social Worker and psychotherapist committed to supporting individuals, couples, and families in reconnecting with themselves and creating meaningful change in their lives.

Melissa believes that healing unfolds through deep self-awareness, emotional processing, and reconnecting with the wisdom of the body. Her work, using evidenced-based interventions, emphasizes embodiment — gently helping clients slow down, tune into their internal experiences, and develop a more compassionate and authentic relationship with themselves.

Through this process, clients are supported in moving beyond simply coping and toward living with greater clarity, resilience, and a deeper sense of thriving.

Each therapist on the team has been thoughtfully selected for their warmth, clinical skill, and shared commitment to supporting clients with compassion, care, and respect for each person's unique journey.

Our Team

Sarah Sallows

she/her

Registered Social Worker

Book A Session

Sarah is a Registered Social Worker who provides counselling to individuals seeking support with anxiety, emotional regulation, trauma, and major life transitions. She brings over 1,200 hours of clinical mental health experience supporting clients through therapy and career guidance.

Before entering social work, Sarah spent over 20 years as a hairstylist and 10 years in restaurant management — experiences that strengthened her ability to connect with people from all walks of life. Her approach is warm, genuine, and deeply relational.

Sarah's work is trauma-informed and guided by the principles of safety, trust, collaboration, and empowerment. She integrates a range of therapeutic approaches including CBT, DBT, ACT, Internal Family Systems (IFS), Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), Narrative Therapy, and attachment-based perspectives, while also incorporating mind–body approaches.

As a mother of three teenagers and stepmother of three more, Sarah understands the complexities of parenting, blended families, and navigating major life transitions.

  • Trauma and emotional regulation
