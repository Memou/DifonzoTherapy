Phone Chat : new clients
15 min block
Phone Chat - New clients, please start here. The most important consideration in therapy is fit with the Therapist. This is both about feeling comfortable with a Therapist and also feeling confident that they have the skills to support in your healing process. Therapists also must feel like they have the right service to offer you.
Initial Assessment
50 minute session time
Initial Assessment - If we’ve chatted on the phone and feel like it’s a good fit, the Initial Assessment is a session to gather history, hear your goals for therapy and address any additional questions, comments or concerns. Very little treatment happens during these appointments as they are mostly about information gathering.
Individual Session
50 minute session time
Individual Psychotherapy Session (phone or video) - Individualized, tailored treatment that will address either supporting you to contextualize your lived experience, learn a new skill or witness and hear a part of you that is needing to be heard. To your preference either phone or video.
