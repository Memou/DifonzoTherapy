Most people come to therapy because they know that they have so much more to about themselves and so much more to give the world. Therapy is about looking at our own darkness so that we can shine a light on it and live in a brighter world. It's like being scared of monsters under the bed but getting out a flashlight to see that it's just some old dirty laundry and boxes of things you meant to de-clutter. Therapy is the flashlight. Compassion and Dignity are the batteries. It's time to look. So you can know what to keep and what to let go of. So you can live in a brighter world of what is actually YOU.
